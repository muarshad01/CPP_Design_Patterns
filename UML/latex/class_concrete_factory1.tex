\doxysubsection{Detailed Description}
Concrete Factories produce a family of products that belong to a single variant. The factory guarantees that resulting products are compatible. Note that signatures of the Concrete Factory\textquotesingle{}s methods return an abstract product, while inside the method a concrete product is instantiated.
